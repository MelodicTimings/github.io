⚠️ DISCLAIMER ⚠️

This site is designed for individuals over the age of 18 with the purpose of providing educational information and resources to people and does NOT provide professional medical advice!

Last Updated: 31/07/2024

We're committed to supporting the transgender community worldwide by providing valuable information and resources on DIY HRT (Hormone Replacement Therapy).
Our goal is to help individuals who are unfortunately unable to get HRT through official channels with the knowledge needed to pursue DIY hormone therapy safely.

Please note that while we offer guidance and educational resources, we do not sell hormones or medical supplies.
We also do not take responsibility for any actions you take with the information procured from this website.

Any issues, suggestions or general questions feel free to contact us by sending an email to HRTCenterINFO@proton.me
