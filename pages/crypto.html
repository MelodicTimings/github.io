What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that doesn't rely on banks to verify transactions. It’s a peer-to-peer system that can enable anyone anywhere to send and receive payments. Instead of being physical money carried around and exchanged in the real world, cryptocurrency payments exist purely as digital entries to an online database describing specific transactions. When you transfer cryptocurrency funds, the transactions are recorded in a public ledger. Cryptocurrency is stored in digital wallets.

Source: Kaspersky

Important Things to Know Before Buying Cryptocurrency

You WILL need to be over the age of 18 and have a valid photographic ID that is accepted by the exchanges

Do NOT transfer funds from the custodial wallet (A wallet that is controlled by an exchange) into a vendor/suppliers wallet

Transfer your funds into a non-custodial wallet (A wallet that is controlled by you) to prevent BLOCKS on your transactions or wallets!

Easiest Method on Buying Cryptocurrency

So you are looking to buy cryptocurrency, this step-by-step guide will show you the easiest method that should work for most people. For the method we will be using MoonPay as our exchange and Exodus as our personal wallet.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

MoonPay x Exodus Method

1) Firstly Navigate to MoonPay in the app store on website and scan the QR code for a download.


2) Once the application has been installed open it up, you will be prompted to continue with a Google account or an email. Select whichever you want.


3) Now that your account has been created you shall start the verification process by clicking the big purple button labeled "Start Verification".


4) After entering your Name, DOB and Address you will be prompted to upload pictures of your ID front and back along with a face scan.


5) While waiting on MoonPay to verify your account you can set up exodus wallet in the meantime. Navigate to the app store and search for "Exodus" and install the app.


6) Upon opening the application you will be prompted with "I have a Wallet" and "Create Wallet" select "Create Wallet".


7) You will now be brought to the wallet home page but before starting I highly recommend backing up your wallet! To do this you will 4 buttons at the bottom, click the 4th one.


8) You should now see "Settings" button, click into it and then select "Backup". After clicking on backup you will be prompted a checkbox with dialog "I understand the risks of sharing my secret phrase". I recommend saving these 12 words somewhere as it will be used in the case you lose access to your wallet. With these 12 words you will be able to recover your wallets.


9) After setting up security on your wallet navigate back to the first page you should see "Receive" and "Buy" buttons. Click on "Receive" and select the cryptocurrency wallet address you want (In this example it will be Bitcoin/BTC). This is the wallet address you will be depositing to from MoonPay as you will have full control over this and won't be limited or blocked.


10) Now that you have your personal wallet setup you can go back to MoonPay to buy cryptocurrency. Hoping that your ID has been verified you should see "Buy" with a + icon on the main screen click it. You will be prompted to buy an amount and a drop-down menu to select which coin you want. (Best are LTC/BTC as they are widely accepted LTC has lower fees than BTC so always prioritize it over BTC)


11) After selecting "Continue" you will be asked to select a wallet address, as in a previous step in the Exodus wallet you can copy and paste your wallet address into the top bar HOWEVER if you don't feel like it might not work you can choose "Your Account" which will be the wallet MoonPay controls.


12) After selecting your wallet address and selecting "Continue" you will be redirected into a web browser for payment details. Here you will just enter your information, to note your payment might get blocked by your bank which is fairly common especially within the UK and EU. To circumvent this I recommend signing up for a service called "Revolut" they have virtual cards that can be used in place of your main debit/credit card. (Jump to section on how to set it up)


13) Once you have confirmed your payment (If you are using Revolut payment method you will be prompted in the app to verify and allow the payment/withdrawal) you will have to wait till the transaction on the blockchain is completed. Depending on the coin and trading volume this could take minutes to hours to be fully verified and transferable to other wallets. If you didn't initially transfer you crypto into your personal wallet on Exodus and into the MoonPay wallet you can transfer a small amount into your personal wallet to make sure you can receive it before transferring it all over.

To do this from the home screen on MoonPay app click the three dots on the coin you bought and then "Send" you will be redirected to a browser.


14) Now you can send an amount of any crypto, or you can swap it to fiat currency and send a certain amount. Fees are already included afterward, so you don't need to worry about any calculations.


15) Now that you have funds in exodus wallet you can send them to the wallet address for payment of products. To do so, navigate to the home screen and whatever coin you have swipe right and "Send".


16) From here you can enter the exact amount listed on the checkout page you won't get limited by decimal amounts like you would on MoonPay and some other exchanges. After entering your amount click "Enter" and then you will be prompted to paste the address or scan a QR code. Then click "Send" and the funds with be put on the blockchain to be confirmed which can take anywhere from a few minutes to a couple of hours depending on the coin and trading volume.


Setting up Revolut

So your payments are getting blocked by your bank? Well thankfully this is where my step-by-step guide to using Revoult comes into play a free simple to set up payment interface.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1) Search for "Revolut" on the app store and install it.


2) Open the App and signup. Complete the account creation and verify details.


3) Once you have you account created and are at the home screen click "Add Money" (You won't be adding money just adding your bank card/account) you should now see "Add debit card" click into there. Look for an option to add your method easiest is to use a debit/credit card. Once your method is linked return to the home screen.


4) Now return to the home screen and on the top right you should see a card icon, click into there you should now see "Cards" and "Add new" Click "Add new"


5) Make sure to select "Virtual" for the card type. You will then select a colour, name and a tag if prompted to add to Google Pay you can add or skip. You should now have a card you can click "Show details" to get the information, this card details will be used on MoonPay or any storefront you wish.


6) Now that should be Revolut setup to use for online purchases, and you should not be getting blocked by your bank for crypto purchases. If you cannot find the card details just remember from the home screen, top right-click the card icon and select the card you created you will then be able to see the details.
